# Who this workshop is for

- Students who want to put real websites and web apps on the internet
- Teams shipping portfolios, CRUD apps, dashboards, Next.js apps, and small APIs
- People who want the simplest path to a public URL, CI, previews, and a custom domain
- Not a deep infrastructure class about VMs, Kubernetes, or cloud architecture theory
